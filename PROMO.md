# WOLF — Telegram promo playbook

No Telegram Premium needed. Growth = buying/swapping posts in other trading
channels, with every join tracked back to the channel that sent it.

## The loop
1. Shortlist gold/FX channels (5k–50k members, active views). DM the admin,
   ask their promo-post price (typical: $10–100) — or offer a free cross-promo.
2. In our bot (admin only):  `/ad <code> [gold|fx|all]`
   e.g. `/ad goldhub24 gold` -> the bot sends back the finished ad post:
   WOLF logo image + copy + a join link tagged with that code.
3. Forward it to the channel admin (or copy image+text). They post it.
4. Every person who joins via that post is recorded under `promo-<code>`.
   You get a real-time ping per join; `/promostats` shows totals per channel.
5. Double down on channels that convert, drop the ones that don't.

## Rules of thumb
- One unique code per channel per campaign (goldhub24, fxlounge-jul...).
- Free cross-promos first, paid tests $20–50 each, 3–5 channels per round.
- Judge by /promostats joins per $ — not by the channel's member count.

## Why not the official Telegram Ads platform?
Direct access needs a ~€2M deposit; resellers start ~$150+ but financial/
signals content is a restricted ad category and often gets rejected. Bought
channel posts have no review, full branding (our logo, buttons, full copy),
and are the standard route in this niche. Revisit resellers when there's
budget to test $150+ on mini-app ad networks.
