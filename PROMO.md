# STAALWAG / WOLF — Telegram promo playbook

No Telegram Premium needed. Growth = buying/swapping posts in other trading
channels, with every join tracked back to the channel that sent it.

Managed from Telegram by the in-process marketing bot (admin only):
`/mkt` overview · `/addch` track a channel · `/chst` move its status ·
`/channels` pipeline · `/followup` chase quiet admins · `/report` (auto every
Monday) what converts · `/copy [gold|fx|all]` ad copy · `/ad <code> [desk]`
build the branded ad · `/promostats` joins per channel.

## 1. Find channels
- **tgstat.com** — search: forex, gold, XAUUSD, forex signals. Shows subs,
  **average post views**, growth. Main tool.
- **telemetr.io** — spot fake-sub channels.
- **Telegram in-app search** — same terms; join a few to see how promos look.

## 2. Vet before you spend
- **Views ÷ subs ≥ 10–20%.** 50k subs but 800 views/post = fake. Skip.
- **Posts wins AND losses.** All-wins = faked results, dead/bot audience.
- **Audience fit** — gold/FX retail, English, ideally your timezone.
- **Active in last 24–48h.**
- **First-round size:** 5k–40k subs (admins answer, rates $10–60).

## 3. Outreach DMs (copy-paste to the channel admin)
Paid:
> Hi — I run STAALWAG, a gold & FX intel desk. Love your channel's engagement.
> Do you offer paid promo posts? I'd share a short branded post (logo + tracked
> join link) to my free channel. What's your rate for one post, and any slot
> this week?

Free cross-promo:
> Hey — I run STAALWAG (gold & FX desk), similar audience to yours. Interested
> in a cross-promo swap? You post mine, I post yours — free reach for both.
> Happy to go first.

## 4. Track every channel (one code ties the whole chain)
1. `/addch goldpips24 @goldpips 30usd 18k subs`   ← start tracking
2. `/chst goldpips24 contacted`                    ← after you DM
3. `/ad goldpips24 gold`                            ← bot builds the branded ad
4. forward the ad to the admin → they post it
5. `/chst goldpips24 posted`
6. joins auto-count under `promo-goldpips24`; `/channels` + `/report` show
   which channels convert. `/copy gold` for fresh angles if they want a variant.

## 5. First-week plan
- List 10 channels on tgstat that pass the vetting bar (mix gold + FX).
- `/addch` all 10.
- Send 3–4 free cross-promo DMs + 3–4 paid DMs ($20–50 each).
- Read `/promostats` after 48h. Rebook only what converts. Judge by joins
  per $, never by member count.

## Why not the official Telegram Ads platform?
Direct access needs a ~€2M deposit; resellers start ~$150+ but financial/
signals content is a restricted ad category and often gets rejected. Bought
channel posts have no review, full branding (logo, buttons, full copy), and
are the standard route in this niche. Revisit resellers when there's budget
to test $150+ on mini-app ad networks.
